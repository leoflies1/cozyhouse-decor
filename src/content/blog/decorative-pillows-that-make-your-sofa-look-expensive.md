## How to Build the Perfect Pillow Arrangement

You've got 13 options — here's how to combine them without overthinking it:

**The "Looks Like I Tried" Setup (5 pillows):**
- Back row: 2x chocolate brown velvet + 1x vintage paisley center
- Front row: 1x bouclé + 1x round bolster

**The Full Luxe Stack (7-8 pillows):**
- Back row: 2x dark gray velvet + earth tone tribal patterns
- Middle: geometric embroidered lumbar + abstract art piece
- Front: shearling faux fur + boho tufted tassels

**The Minimalist Sweet Spot (3 pillows):**
- 1x bouclé ivory + 1x chocolate brown velvet + 1x round bolster

The rule: vary your textures, vary your shapes, stick to 2-3 colors max. Done.

## One More Thing

Don't buy all 13 at once. Start with **three** that cover your bases: one smooth texture (velvet), one bold pattern (abstract art or earth tones), and one unique shape (the round bolster or the daisy). That combo alone will transform your sofa. Add the rest gradually as you figure out your style.

The best pillow arrangements aren't planned — they evolve. Start somewhere, live with it for a week, then swap, add, or remove. Your couch will tell you what it needs.
