A bathroom renovation can cost thousands. But the truth is, the changes that make the biggest visual difference are also the cheapest. Here are twelve ways to completely transform your bathroom for under €50 — no contractor required.

## 1. Repaint the Vanity Cabinet

A tired vanity is often the ugliest thing in a bathroom, and paint fixes it in an afternoon. Choose a matte finish in sage green, navy, or soft white for a 2026 update. <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=cabinet+paint+kit+bathroom" rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank">Bathroom cabinet paint kits</a> include everything you need — primer, paint, and topcoat.

## 2. Replace the Mirror

That basic builder-grade rectangle mirror is bringing down your entire bathroom. Swap it for a round mirror, an arched mirror, or even a framed vintage-style piece. <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=round+bathroom+mirror+wall+mounted" rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank">Round bathroom mirrors</a> start at around €20 and change the whole vibe.

## 3. Add a Shower Curtain with Pattern

If you have a shower-tub combo, the curtain is the largest decorative element in the room. Make it count. Choose a textured fabric curtain in a pattern you love — terracotta stripes, olive green botanical, or cream waffle weave. <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=waffle+weave+shower+curtain" rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank">Textured shower curtains</a> look far more expensive than they are.

## 4. Swap the Hardware

New cabinet knobs and drawer pulls cost literally a few euros each but make the vanity look custom. Matte black, brushed brass, or antique bronze — pick one finish and commit. <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=matte+black+cabinet+knobs+bathroom" rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank">Matte black bathroom knobs</a> are the most popular choice in 2026.

## 5. Add a Bath Mat with Texture

Ditch the thin, boring mat and upgrade to something thick and luxurious underfoot. A textured bath mat in natural materials like cotton, bamboo, or memory foam makes the bathroom feel spa-like. <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=luxury+bath+mat+thick" rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank">Luxury bath mats</a> start at under €15.

## 6. Install New Towel Hooks

Towel bars are fine, but hooks add personality and are more practical for small spaces. Try <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=brass+towel+hooks+bathroom" rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank">brass towel hooks</a> in a row of three — they look intentional and keep towels off the floor.

## 7. Add Greenery

A bathroom is the perfect environment for plants — the humidity keeps them happy. A small pothos, fern, or air plant on the counter or windowsill breathes life into the space instantly. <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=small+bathroom+plants+low+light" rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank">Low-light bathroom plants</a> thrive even without natural light.

## 8. Change the Showerhead

You'd be amazed how much a new showerhead upgrades the daily experience. A rain-style or handheld showerhead with multiple settings makes your morning routine feel luxurious. <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=rain+showerhead+high+pressure" rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank">High-pressure rain showerheads</a> are easy to install and start at €20.

## 9. Add a Tray for Counter Items

A small decorative tray on the counter — holding your soap, hand cream, and maybe a candle — instantly makes random items look curated and intentional. <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=marble+bathroom+tray" rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank">Marble bathroom trays</a> are elegant and affordable.

## 10. Paint the Wall (Just One)

You don't need to paint all four walls. Pick one accent wall — behind the mirror or the wall the vanity sits on — and paint it a warm, moody color. Deep green, navy, or even a terracotta tone adds incredible depth for just the cost of one sample pot. <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=accent+wall+paint+bathroom" rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank">Small paint samples</a> are perfect for this.

## 11. Add a Small Shelf Above the Toilet

The space above the toilet is almost always wasted. A small floating shelf there can hold a candle, a small plant, and rolled-up towels — adding storage and decor in one move. <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=floating+shelf+above+toilet" rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank">Above-toilet floating shelves</a> come pre-assembled.

## 12. Light a Candle

The simplest upgrade of all. A scented candle on the counter creates atmosphere, masks bathroom odors, and makes the space feel intentional. Choose something fresh — eucalyptus, sea salt, or clean linen. <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=eucalyptus+scented+candle" rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank">Eucalyptus scented candles</a> are a bathroom staple for good reason.

---

Total budget for all twelve: well under €50 if you pick and choose. Start with the mirror and the paint — those two alone will make your bathroom look like a completely different room.
